🔌 API ENDPOINTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SAÚDE
  GET /health                         → {status: "ok"}

LEADS
  GET /api/leads                      → Lista todos os leads
  GET /api/leads/:phone               → Detalhes do lead + mensagens
  GET /api/leads/hot                  → Apenas hot leads (score > 80)

WEBHOOKS (INCOMING)
  POST /webhooks/uazapi/message       → Mensagem WhatsApp
  POST /webhooks/chatwoot/message-created  → Mensagem Chatwoot

📊 FLUXO DE FUNCIONAMENTO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. Cliente envia mensagem via WhatsApp
       ↓
  2. UAZAPI webhook em /webhooks/uazapi/message
       ↓
  3. Criar/atualizar Lead no banco
       ↓
  4. Salvar Mensagem
       ↓
  5. Classificar Intenção (OpenAI)
       ├─ BUY_NOW    → +30 pontos
       ├─ SUPPORT    → +10 pontos
       └─ TRIAGE     → +0 pontos
       ↓
  6. Atualizar Score
       ↓
  7. Determinar Status
       ├─ TRIAGE    (score < 50)
       ├─ HOT       (score > 80 + BUY_NOW)
       ├─ FOLLOW_UP (sem resposta 24h)
       ├─ COLD      (sem resposta 7d)
       └─ ARCHIVED  (manual)
       ↓
  8. Aplicar Labels no Chatwoot
       ↓
  9. Jobs Automáticos
       ├─ Follow-up 24h → Enviar lembretes
       └─ Cold Storage 7d → Arquivar inativos
